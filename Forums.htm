We have not yet decided on the best place for our forums.  So, until we make that decision, we will conduct forum dicussions using our MicroRealities mailing lists.

To join a mailing list, simply click on a game image - the magic user, spaceman, or dinosaur.  When your pre-addressed message comes up, simply type the word "join" (without the quotes) on the subject line, and send the message.  If your email program does not automatically launch when you click on a game image, then you can manually mail your request to 'pravos@mrgaming.com' (or 'opsrav' or 'voraps', depending on your interest).

You should receive a confirmation message, that will contain a zipped file, within 3 working days.  The file will have the most recent discussions.  Note: Each message that is contained in the zipped file will not be in HTML, but will be in a simple ASCII format.

To remove yourself from a mailing list, simply click on a game image and type the word "remove" on the subject line.  You should receive confirmation and removal, within a few working days.
