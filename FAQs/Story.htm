Can you tell me more of the "Pravos" story line?

Not really, because most of the details of the "in game" histories, ancient heroes, founding communities, and so on, are still being developed.  Many of the Grand Quests, Wild Lands, Unknown Ruins, Lair Creatures, and so forth do exist, but these are subjects that will be discovered "in game" first. 

Because Pravos will have an actual time-line, it means that all "in game" information and events really have happened.  Or said another way, all books, scrolls, and visions that tell of histories, heroes, and events actually did occur in the game, by real characters (player or computer controlled), including "The Great Joining".

Now, is the best time to offer suggestions or ideas for the story line.  However, up until the public BETA begins, tweaks and additions to the story line will occur.  So, join the discussions in the Pravos Forum.
