MicroRealities Gaming

Host your own VaPORS reality.


(FUTURE) Host a VaPORS game - There are plans to offer opportunities to ISPs for hosting games primarily to support their own customers. The speeds possible within an ISPs Intranet, are not currently possible on the Internet. Therefore, once VaPORS games are accepted by the marketplace, an ISP could offer their customers many features requiring very high bandwidths. Some features currently being designed for development include virtual-voice-overs, virtual-play-streams, and virtual-player-morphs.

(FUTURE) Franchise a VaPORS game - There are plans to eventually offer franchise-like opportunities to experienced VaPORS players and designers, who have demonstrated the ability to deliver and support MicroRealities plans and policies. Franchises will have their own unique story lines not found on other hosts.
