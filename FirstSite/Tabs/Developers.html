MicroRealities Gaming

Mold VaPORS to your reality.


Apply for "artistic" membership - Artists, musicians, and sound effect specialists are welcome to submit their VaPORS visions. When the "public gallery" goes live, a few rules must be followed to keep your membership active, but not so for your "private gallery".

Apply for "designer" membership - Create and submit VaPORS game scenarios. A limited number of "designer" memberships will be approved; allowing access to some MicroRealities tools. Note that the public "submits" scenarios, and the "designer" creates them.

Apply for "developer" membership - A small and limited number of "registered" and "unregistered" memberships will be approved during the "alpha" phase (Jan-July 2000). A requirement list and non-disclosure agreement will be emailed to the applicant.
