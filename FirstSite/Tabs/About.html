MicroRealities Gaming

The primary intent of MicroRealities is to change the way gaming software is created, written, and supported.

The primary intent of the VaPORS project is to enable and help people exercise their creativity in ways constructive and cooperative. This motivation offers not only a diverse and rich "in game" experience, but will hopefully create a responsive and inspired community of VaPORS professionals, enthusiasts, and contributors. The secondary intent of the project is to create a forum for this community to publish, discuss, and financially support their VaPORS products and services.

Previous to this time (March 2, 1999), no outside investing was considered. This decision to wait, made a few years ago, was to ensure that the concept of VaPORS would not be lost or diluted along it's way to implementation. Now that the main concepts are "proven", funding will be addressed next year, once the alpha is complete.

Because the VaPORS design is not tied to any particular genre and offers expansions quite far reaching, it never the less will first exist in the "fantasy" genre. But it will not follow the familiar "Dungeons and Dragons", or other board game, characterizations. Instead it will be based on character perception and pseudo-physics to set the stage for discovery and predictable interactions with environments of "fantasy", "horror", "sci-fi", etc. Note that if a designer chooses to, they can restrict and turn off many world features in order to make a classic "Dungeons and Dragons" style game.
