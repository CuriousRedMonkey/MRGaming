Apply for "Investor" membership - There are three plans for the potential investor. Crudely stated, they are "Subscriber", "Little Guy", and "Big Guy". The "Subscriber" approach is an audacious plan that will offer a limited number of low-cost monthly beta subscriptions this fall 2000, which is when the first VaPORS game is expected to enter beta. For this plan to work, a minimum number of subscribers would have to exist to support the costs of a high bandwidth 24x7 line, peripheral and direct services, etc. For this early support, the investor will receive "in game" compensations as listed in the "Subscriber" membership agreement. Concerning the "Guy" plans, mentioned above, the investor can invest lump sums and expect returns consistent with industry standards.

Apply for "Advertiser" membership - There will be three ways to advertise at MicroRealities; "broadcast", "geographic", and "specialty". Each approach is restricted to a medium: "broadcast" to web sites, "geographic" to the network interface, and "specialty" to a VaPORS game. Your request for membership will return an email that will further define these terms and restrictions, however a brief explanation will now follow. We have seen "broadcast" on TV, billboards, and the internet. The public has no control over its presence. "Geographic" is about local businesses advertising to local people. The public can control its presence. "Specialty" has probably never been seen before, because of the design and nature of a VaPORS game. It is completely transparent as a "real world" advertisement and fits within a game's role-played environment.
