MicroRealities Gaming

Experience a new on-line game reality.


A VaPORS game - Game images represent actual game objects. Therefore, anything your creature sees it can interact with, modify, or ultimately create. Furthermore, the way you play your creature will determine its personality. This is because your game play choices develop your creature's "habits of behavior". You interact with a VaPORS game via an internet enabled "client application".

A VaPORS client - Currently the "client application" uses a simple overhead 2D perspective. However, it can be configured for any perspective (1st person, overhead, angled) or style (2D, 3D). Non-programmers can easily tailor the client by selecting from menus. The artistically inclined can add new, or replace existing, images, sounds, and music.
